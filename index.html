About the Club

An inclusive reading and learning community guided by shared values.

The Read, Learn and Shine Club is based under the Department of Library and Information Science, NEHU Shillong. The club promotes literacy, knowledge dissemination, intellectual engagement, and an inclusive culture of reading and lifelong learning.

M

Motto

Nurturing Young Minds.

V

Vision

To create an informed, literate, and intellectually empowered community.

M

Mission

To promote reading culture, critical thinking, and access to knowledge resources.

Core Objectives

What the club works to achieve

Promote Reading Habits

Encourage reading habits among children, youth, and adults.

Improve Access

Facilitate access to books, digital resources, and educational materials.

Organize Learning Programs

Conduct workshops, seminars, reading sessions, and literacy campaigns.

Collaborate Widely

Work with schools, libraries, and community organizations.

Support Innovation

Support research and innovation in literacy and education.
