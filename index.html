About the Club

An inclusive reading and learning community guided by shared values.

The Read, Learn and Shine Club operates as a non-profit, voluntary, and non-political organization. It recognizes the transformative role of literacy, knowledge dissemination, and intellectual engagement in societal development while fostering an inclusive culture of reading and lifelong learning.

Mission

Core Objectives

What the club works to achieve

Promote Reading Habits

Encourage reading habits among children, youth, and adults.

Improve Access

Facilitate access to books, digital resources, and educational materials.

Organize Learning Programs

Conduct workshops, seminars, reading sessions, and literacy campaigns.

Collaborate Widely

Work with schools, libraries, and community organizations.

Support Innovation

Support research and innovation in literacy and education.

Membership

Open to people with a genuine interest in literacy, education, or community development.

Membership is open to individuals who demonstrate genuine interest in literacy, education, or community development, including those with professional qualifications in Library and Information Science such as MLIS or BLIS.
